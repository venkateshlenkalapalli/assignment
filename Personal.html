Personal

Your personal statement should include a brief overview of who you are, your strengths and any work experience and/or education you've got. Be sure to include skills you've gained, such as time management, customer service, teamwork, computer skills etc.The personal statement is also referred to as a career summary or personal mission statement. This is used to grab the attention of the recruiter or hiring manager and summarizes essential experiences or training that you can bring to this position.
